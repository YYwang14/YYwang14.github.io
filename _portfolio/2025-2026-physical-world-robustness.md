Studying physical-world adversarial patch robustness against object detectors, with emphasis on how attacks survive the physical-to-digital pipeline from actuation and environmental transmission to sensing and preprocessing.

Work includes simulation and physical testbeds involving YOLOv5, CARLA, Gazebo, ROS2, PX4/drone setups, real camera capture, and printed adversarial objects and patches.
